## Limitations

The displayed outputs are curated and do not prove parser execution. Malformed data, multiline input, encoding, truncation, escaping variants, and parser-version behavior are not tested. Review the [complete configuration](/data-transformation/parse-logs/complete-parser) before adapting it.
